About

Charlotte-based infrastructure leader. I run platform engineering and operational resilience for Meridian's banking estate — the systems behind 420+ branches and everything our customers touch on their phones. This past year has come down to one question I keep getting from leadership, and now from our examiners: when something goes wrong in a release, how fast do we know — before a customer ever does? After a rough Friday in March, that stopped being theoretical. Resilience isn't a line on my roadmap anymore; it's the mandate. We're a more complicated shop than people assume. Two core-banking platforms still running side by side from the Heritage integration, a fast-growing cloud footprint, and a monitoring stack that grew by accretion rather than design. Simplifying all of that is most of my job right now. Before Meridian I spent six years leading infrastructure at a top-10 US bank, so regulated, high-stakes environments are home for me. I came up the technical track and still care more about how something behaves at 2am than how it looks in a demo. I mentor with Women in Tech here in Charlotte and sit on the board of a local STEM nonprofit — always happy to talk with people earlier in their careers. If you're a vendor: I respect a sharp point of view, but please don't open with a product tour. Lead with my world and we'll get along fine.
