About

I run the SRE team that keeps Meridian's banking platforms reliable around the clock — the systems behind 420+ branches and a mobile app a lot of people depend on. Came up through platform engineering, so I care about day-2 reality, not vision slides: what actually breaks at 2am, how long it takes us to notice, and how fast we put it back. Right now "day-2" means wrangling two core-banking systems that don't log the same way, about ten monitoring tools that don't agree with each other, and a Kubernetes buildout we're standing up at the same time. We're under a hard mandate to cut MTTR. The current tooling won't get us there, and I'm not shy about saying so. I'm an engineer at heart. If a vendor tells me their thing is "AI-powered," my next three questions are: causal or correlation, what's the false-positive rate, and what does it do to my ingest bill. Bring me real numbers and a day-in-the-life of my team and you'll have my attention. Bring me a keynote and you won't. Outside work I'm on a bike most weekends — did the Assault on Mount Mitchell last year and I'm still recovering emotionally. Also maintain a home lab that mostly exists to break things on purpose. Reports into Rachel Morgan. Always happy to talk shop with other SRE and platform folks.
